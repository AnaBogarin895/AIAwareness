Prompting Frameworks

Choose the right framework for your needs

RISEN Framework

Role, Input, Scenario, Example, Narrowing - A structured approach for generating clear, high-quality AI outputs by defining the AI's role, clarifying input, specifying steps, supporting with evidence, and adding nuance.

Role

Define the AI's expertise and perspective

Input

Provide clear context and data

Scenario

Set the situation and constraints

Example

Show desired output format

Narrowing

Add specific requirements and tone

Best For:

Legal Analysis Market Research Financial Analysis Problem Solving

Example Prompt:

Role: You are a senior financial analyst
Input: Q3 revenue data showing 15% decline
Scenario: Board meeting tomorrow, need executive summary
Example: "Revenue declined due to [reason]. Recommend [action]"
Narrowing: Keep under 200 words, focus on actionable insights

RACE Framework

Role, Action, Context, Example - Craft prompts by specifying who the AI is, who it's responding for, the situation, and desired output format.

Role

Define AI's expertise and audience

Action

Specify what needs to be done

Context

Provide situational background

Example

Show desired format or style

Best For:

Product Optimization Marketing Campaigns Content Generation Operations Execution

Example Prompt:

Role: You are a digital marketing specialist
Action: Write 3 subject lines for a product launch email
Context: Launching a new AI feature for small business owners next week.
Example: For Example: Meet Your New Smart Assistant Launching Tuesday

CARE Framework

Context, Action, Response, Evaluate - An iterative framework for improving AI outputs by providing context, asking clearly, refining responses, and evaluating usefulness.

Context

Ground the task with background

Action

Ask clearly and specifically

Response

Review and refine the output

Evaluate

Assess and iterate for improvement

Best For:

Analytical Reports Reduces AI Hallucinations 60-70% Complex Problem Solving

Example Prompt:

Context: We're deploying a new backend API and facing inconsistent response times
Action: Analyze the possible causes
Response: Provide a bulleted list of 3 likely causes with one-line explanations
Evaluate: Suggest one monitoring tool to help verify performance

CRISPE Framework

Capacity, Role, Instructions, Style, Persona, and Examples - A quality-check framework to ensure content and campaigns are strategically sound and audience-focused.

Capacity

Define AI's capabilities

Role

Set expertise & identity

Instructions

Specify the task

Style

Define tone and writing approach

Persona

Specify target audience characteristics

Examples

Provide reference formats and samples

Best For:

Digital Marketing Copywriting Technical Documentation Brand Communication UI/UX Optimization

Example Prompt:

Consistent: Maintain our friendly, professional brand voice
Relevant: Focus on small business owners' time constraints
Integrated: Align with our Q4 product launch campaign
Style: Use conversational tone with urgency elements
Persona: Target entrepreneurs aged 30-45 with limited tech knowledge
Examples: "Save 3 hours daily with automated workflows" format

Role-Based Prompt Examples

Tailored prompts for every department in your organization

Marketing

Campaign Copy

"You are a creative copywriter. Write 5 different email subject lines for our Black Friday sale, targeting millennial shoppers. Make them urgent but not spammy."

Social Media Strategy

"You are a social media strategist. Create a 7-day Instagram content plan for a B2B SaaS company. Include post types, captions, and hashtag suggestions."

Audience Personas

"You are a market researcher. Based on this customer survey data, create 3 detailed buyer personas with demographics, pain points, and preferred communication channels."

Sales

Cold Outreach

"You are a B2B sales expert. Write a personalized LinkedIn message to a VP of Operations at a manufacturing company. Our product helps reduce operational costs. Keep it under 300 characters."

Objection Handling

"You are a sales coach. Help me respond to this objection: 'Your solution is too expensive.' Provide 3 different response strategies with examples."

Proposal Writing

"You are a proposal writer. Create an executive summary for a $50K software implementation project. Highlight ROI, timeline, and risk mitigation. Keep it to one page."

Product

User Stories

"You are a product manager. Convert this feature request into 5 detailed user stories with acceptance criteria: 'Users want to export their data in multiple formats.'"

Competitive Analysis

"You are a product strategist. Analyze these 3 competitors' pricing models and suggest positioning for our premium tier. Format as strengths/weaknesses/opportunities table."

Feature Prioritization

"You are a product owner. Score these 8 feature requests using RICE framework (Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort). Present as a prioritized table with justifications."

Engineering

Code Review

"You are a senior software engineer. Review this Python function for performance issues, security vulnerabilities, and code style. Suggest specific improvements with explanations."

Architecture Design

"You are a solutions architect. Design a scalable microservices architecture for an e-commerce platform handling 1M+ daily users. Include database, caching, and API gateway considerations."

Documentation

"You are a technical writer. Create comprehensive API documentation for this REST endpoint, including examples, error codes, and rate limiting information."

Human Resources

Job Descriptions

"You are an HR specialist. Create a compelling job description for a Senior UX Designer role. Include responsibilities, qualifications, and company culture elements. Make it inclusive and appealing."

Performance Reviews

"You are an HR manager. Help me write constructive feedback for an employee who shows great technical skills but struggles with communication. Provide specific examples and improvement suggestions."

Training Programs

"You are a learning and development specialist. Design a 30-day onboarding program for remote software developers. Include milestones, check-ins, and resource recommendations."

Leadership

Strategic Planning

"You are a strategy consultant. Help me analyze these market trends and create a 3-year strategic plan for our SaaS company. Include growth opportunities, risks, and resource allocation."

Change Management

"You are a change management expert. Create a communication plan for announcing a major organizational restructuring. Address concerns, timeline, and support resources."

Investor Relations

"You are a CFO preparing for investor meetings. Create an executive summary of our Q3 performance highlighting key metrics, achievements, and forward-looking statements."

Advanced Prompting Best Practices

Level up your AI interactions with these pro techniques

Be Specific

Instead of "summarize this," try "create a 3-bullet executive summary focusing on financial impact."

Set the Tone

Add personality: "Write like a Gen Z influencer" or "Use a consultative, executive tone."

Format Constraints

Specify output: "Format as a table," "Write in bullet points," "Create a JSON structure."

Iterate & Refine

Follow up with: "Make it shorter," "Add more technical detail," "Simplify for beginners."

Advanced Techniques for Power Users

Chain of Thought

Guide AI through step-by-step reasoning

"Let's think step by step: 1) First, analyze the data 2) Then, identify patterns 3) Finally, provide recommendations"

Few-Shot Learning

Provide examples to guide AI behavior

"Example 1: Input Output
Example 2: Input Output
Now do: [Your input]"

System Messages

Set persistent context and constraints

"You are an expert analyst. Always cite sources. Be concise but thorough. Use data to support claims."

Common Prompting Pitfalls

Avoid these mistakes for better AI interactions

Don't Do This

"Help me with this"

Too vague - AI doesn't know what "this" is or how to help

"Write something about marketing"

No specific task, format, or outcome defined

"Make it better"

"Better" is subjective - specify what improvements you want

Single long paragraph with multiple requests

Confusing and hard to process - break into clear sections

Do This Instead

"You are a marketing expert. Analyze this email campaign's open rates and suggest 3 specific improvements."

Clear role, task, and expected output

"Create a 5-slide presentation outline for our Q4 product launch, targeting enterprise clients."

Specific format, length, and audience defined

"Make this email more persuasive by adding social proof and urgency. Keep it under 200 words."

Specific improvements and constraints provided

Use RISEN or CARE frameworks

Structured approach ensures all elements are covered
